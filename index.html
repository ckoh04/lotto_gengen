How This Generator Works

Lottery Gen Gen creates randomized picks based on public game formats. For Mega Millions-style picks, the generator returns 5 unique numbers from 1-70 plus one Mega Ball from 1-25. For Powerball-style picks, it returns 5 unique numbers from 1-69 plus one Powerball from 1-26.

For Powerball-style generation, you can optionally include a simulated Power Play multiplier and a separate Double Play number set. These options are generated independently to keep the process clear and easy to understand.

This tool is for entertainment only. It does not predict outcomes and does not increase the statistical chance of winning. Always verify official rules, cutoff times, and winning numbers with your state lottery authority.

Quick Start

  • Choose Mega Millions or Powerball using the logo selector.
  • For Powerball, enable Power Play and/or Double Play if desired.
  • Select "Generate Numbers" to create a new random set.
  • Copy your numbers and confirm current draw rules before buying a ticket.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do these numbers improve my odds?

No. Every eligible number combination has the same chance in a fair draw.

Can I use this in any U.S. state?

Use this tool as a helper only. Ticket availability, game options, and deadlines vary by state and retailer.

How often should I generate numbers?

There is no optimal frequency. Use a budget, play responsibly, and never spend money you cannot afford to lose.
